每一部乐章都有尾声（coda）。不是终结，而是在主题结束之后，留给听者回味的那几个小节。

时事评论大多在追逐主题——谁赢了，谁输了，下一步怎样。Coda想做的，是那几个小节：慢下来，把中国、新加坡、欧洲三个信息圈的声音放在一起，看看它们各自在说什么，又在沉默什么。

每周一期。没有广告，没有算法。只有编辑判断。

---EN---

Every musical piece has a coda — not an ending, but a few bars after the main theme, left for the listener to sit with.

Most news commentary chases the theme: who won, who lost, what comes next. Coda wants those extra bars: to slow down, to place the voices of China, Singapore, and Europe side by side, and to notice what each is saying — and what each is silent about.

One issue a week. No ads, no algorithm. Only editorial judgment.
